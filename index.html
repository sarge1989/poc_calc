Calculate the premium for new insurance covers under the Home Protection Scheme.

This calculator is for members who have applied to be insured under the Home Protection Scheme (HPS) for their most recent flat purchase. It estimates the premium they would have to pay in relation to their selected percentage share of coverage.

If you are adjusting your HPS cover, please write to us.

Important notes
  • HPS only covers members who are between the ages of 21 and 65.
  • All fields are mandatory unless otherwise indicated.
