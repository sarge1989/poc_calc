Calculate the premium for insurance under the Home Protection Scheme.

The Home Protection Scheme (HPS) is a mortgage-reducing insurance that protects members and their families against losing their HDB flat in the event of death, terminal illness or total permanent disability.

Coverage under HPS is required when you use your CPF savings to buy a home. Home buyers who use their CPF will be notified to sign up for HPS..

Important notes
  • This calculator only calculates premiums for new HPS applications. To obtain a calculation for HPS cover adjustments, please write to us.
To use this calculator, you will need:
  • Details of your home loan, such as the loan amount, loan term, and type of interest rate.
